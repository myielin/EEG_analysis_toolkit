In this folder there are two EEG samples (sampleEEG) with its respective annotations (sampleEVS).
The EEG recordings were taken from a cognitive task where the person read two texts: one of higher complexity (sample 2) and a simple children's tale (sample 1). 
Annotations are points in time where the person lost the focus of attention while reading. It is formatted as min:sec:ms, being 00:00:00 the onset of EEG recording.
This is a section of a dataset designed for analyzing brain dynamics involved in sustained attention.

General information:
  - EEG device: Open BCI Ganglion 
  - Number of channels: 4 (left/right frontal and parietal)
  - Sampling frequency: 200hZ
